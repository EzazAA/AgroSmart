HOW AND WHY

AgroSmart Hardware and Software

The need for tools to farm has always increased and there is no alternative yet to farming, These days maintaining a healthy environment for the crops is very neccesary. Aligining with this, AgroSmart have been developed

Inspiration

AgroSmart have been inspired bu Anubhav shivam nath's SIMDS-Hydro Aligining with that model, AgroSmart have been made cheaper and way more features have been acomplished and deployed

Functioning

The model functions on an ESP32, connecting the device with the android app, sending and receiving data and controliing the water flow based on dryness of soil.

How to Use

Using AgroSmart is pretty straight forward, Download the app, turn on the device and then connect to the hotspot (AgroSmart WiFi). Open the app and keep refreshing.
