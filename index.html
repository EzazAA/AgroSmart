Inspiration

Agriculture is the backbone of our economy, but farmers face challenges like resource mismanagement, climate changes, and lack of timely information. Agro Smart was developed to fullfil this requirements, It works to send the soil moisture, Humidity , temperature and Light intensity data to a mobile phone app and automatically control water pump. It also has a disease prediction Opyion which cen predict the diseases based on the given symptomns.

Monitoring Sensors

- Soil Moisture Sensor: Tracks moisture levels to prevent overwatering or drought stress.
- Temperature and Humidity Sensor: Records environmental conditions for optimal growth.

Automated Irrigation

The motor controlled by an ESP8266 module automatically irrigates crops based on soil moisture data, ensuring efficient water usage.

Data Transmission

Data collected by the sensors is sent to a mobile application, providing farmers with real-time insights for decision-making.
